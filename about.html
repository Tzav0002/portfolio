About Me

My Story

Hello! My name is Matthew Tzavelas and I am a web developer, photographer & graphic designer based in Ottawa. I specialize in creating graphics such as logos & social media advertisements. In my free time, I enjoy making music, reading and doing macrophotography. I believe that pursuing interests outside of work helps to keep me creatively inspired and motivated.

If you are interested in working with me, please take a look at my portfolio to see examples of my previous work.
